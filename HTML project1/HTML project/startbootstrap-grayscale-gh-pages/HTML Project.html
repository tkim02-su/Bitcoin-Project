Strengths of Bitcoin:
Convenient, Decentralized and Protected.

First, one of the advantages is that Bitcoin is not tangible, which means that it is not contingent on how big or small it is. No matter how far away people are, they can still digitally transport a Bitcoin, or even a fraction of a Bitcoin. Therefore, Bitcoin created a sensational boom among the whole entire world, until the price plunged to its death.

But...Bitcoin is not more than just a bubble

Since the price of Bitcoin is way too capricious, no one knows the future value of Bitcoin and the potential of it being used globally.In the world of economics, supply and demand are major components that shape the society. And, Bitcoin does not fulfill either of them because supply is limited when demand of Bitcoin is debatable in the opinion of professional finance investors.


Meanwhile, Blockchain Technology is...

While the future of Bitcoin and cryptocurrency seems to be unpredictable, blockchain technology has been thriving with a bright future. The fact that Blockchain technology could possibly track down all the products frames the infrastructure of increasing accountability and integrity. And, it exemplifies how infrastructure of blockchain technology is well-developed.
