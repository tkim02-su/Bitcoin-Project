About Our Founder

Taeuk, the CEO and founder, did not set out to build a business. But he was famously just a college student at Syracuse University when he launched the organization in 2017.

He has initiated the cryptocurrency website project in 2018.

Who is he?

Taeuk Kim (born September 30, 1997) is an Korean technology entrepreneur. He left Syracuse University after his junior year to concentrate on the site, the cryptocurrency base of which has grown to worth more than 2 billion dollars.

What did he do?

He was the first to create cryptocurrency website for his IST263 project, and got an A on the course. As of today, with a fortune estimated at $60 billion, he is one of the world's richest people. But because of his youth, he has actually made more money per day of living than anybody else on the planet.

What is he going to do?

He has already succeeded, but he is going to build another organization, an information technology company. The website for his organization will be launching in less than 2 years.
